RAIN VIDEO OVERLAY (primary mode)
==================================
Place a rain overlay video here as: rain.mp4

This should be a video of rain filmed against a BLACK background.
The app uses "Screen" blend mode so black disappears and only the
bright water/droplet reflections are visible over your blurred desktop.

Where to find free rain overlay videos:
  - Pexels: search "rain overlay black background"
  - Pixabay: search "rain overlay"
  - YouTube: search "rain on glass overlay free download" (many creators offer free 4K loops)
  - Videezy: search "rain overlay"

Tips:
  - 1080p or higher looks best
  - Looping videos (10-30 seconds) work perfectly
  - The darker the background, the better the compositing looks
  - "Rain on window" or "rain on glass" overlays look most realistic

Supported formats: .mp4, .avi, .mov, .mkv, .webm

If no video is found, the app falls back to rendered rain drops.

ICON (optional)
===============
Place icon.png here (64x64 or 256x256 PNG with transparent background).
If absent, a blue raindrop icon is auto-generated at startup.
